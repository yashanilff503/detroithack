We're Detroit Hacks.

An upcoming Michigan-based hackathon founded by high school students for high school students with hundreds in prizes.

>What is DetroitHacks?

DetroitHacks is a hackathon that will take place on Sunday June 4 2023. Held at the Novi Public Library in Detroit, this event provides attendees with a valuable opportunity to participate in workshops, gain practical experience, establish connections, and discover more about themselves. DetroitHacks will feature algorithm-based challenges tailored to various programming levels, fostering collaboration among participants. Whether you are interested in programming or technology, this event is an excellent platform to learn and enhance your skills. Attend DetroitHacks and unlock your potential in the world of programming!
